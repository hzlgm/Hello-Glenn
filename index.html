About Me

I believe in people. Ideas don't magically appear by themselves. I believe that you create magic in bouncing off ideas from people, in which you make winning twofold - you win ideas, you win friends. But truth be told, I prefer the sound of the latter. Ideas can fail, but a friend can always buy you coffee.

I enjoy the work of making complex ideas simple for everyday folks. My past life has been an examplary of such as I made investing simple for my clients to understand. But for now, I just want to invest in myself for a while.
